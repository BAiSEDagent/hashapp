# Uniswap

## Why it fits Hashapp
Uniswap is the best concrete value-movement action for Hashapp if we want one live economic action inside the product.

## Product role
Use Uniswap only if it clearly appears in the live flow.
Examples:
- agent swap within scope
- agent buys an asset or rebalances under policy
- a spending action that visibly uses Uniswap routing

## Product value
Gives the app a real “agents that pay” action instead of purely abstract approvals.

## Track alignment
- Agents that pay
- potential partner prize upside

## Rule
Do not include Uniswap just for logo value. It should show up clearly in the user experience or demo narrative.
