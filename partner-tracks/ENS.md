# ENS

## Why it fits Hashapp
ENS improves readability, trust, search, and product polish.

## Product role
Use ENS or Basenames to make:
- agents feel real
- payees feel human-readable
- receipts feel legible
- trusted destinations feel like known entities instead of hex strings
- search feel natural in Activity and trusted destination flows

## Best surfaces
ENS/Basenames are especially valuable in:
- Activity trusted-destination rail
- search bar results
- payee / vendor identity rows
- agent identity display
- receipt detail proof context

## Product value
ENS helps turn Hashapp from crypto tooling into a readable consumer product.
It is also one of the cleanest ways to support the trust layer for:
- trusted destinations
- humans
- agents
- services

## Track alignment
- Agents that trust

## Rule
ENS should appear as identity polish and trust infrastructure, not as a dominant product feature.
