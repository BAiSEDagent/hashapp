# ENS

## Why it fits Hashapp
ENS improves readability, trust, and product polish.

## Product role
Use ENS or Basenames to make:
- agents feel real
- payees feel human-readable
- receipts feel legible
- trusted destinations feel like known entities instead of hex strings

## Product value
ENS helps turn Hashapp from crypto tooling into a readable consumer product.

## Track alignment
- Agents that trust

## Rule
ENS should appear as identity polish and trust infrastructure, not as a dominant product feature.
