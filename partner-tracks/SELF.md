# Self

## Why it fits Hashapp
Self fits if we want a real privacy or selective-disclosure layer for sensitive spending decisions.

## Product role
Potential uses:
- private identity checks
- selective disclosure for trusted spending contexts
- proving a user has permission without revealing more than needed

## Product value
Strengthens the “agents that keep secrets” story.

## Track alignment
- Agents that keep secrets
- Agents that trust

## Rule
Only foreground Self if we implement one clear privacy moment. Do not include it as abstract futureware.
